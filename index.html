About Me

A short introduction

I'm Maximiliano Perez, though I usually go by Max. I'm a fourth-year student at UC San Diego studying Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction. I'm interested in how people think, create, and connect through design and technology. Outside of academics, I play guitar and enjoy spending time under the night sky, which often inspires my curiosity and creativity. I'm especially drawn to work that blends human experience with thoughtful, intuitive design. In the future, I hope to create meaningful, user-centered experiences that feel both impactful and personal.
