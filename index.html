Project 01 Case Study

Overview and User Research

Overview

Messaging apps like WhatsApp often assume users can read English, understand familiar interface terms, and easily access translation tools. For middle-aged and older immigrants with limited technology experience, everyday tasks like reading messages, replying in English, or spotting scams can become confusing and stressful.

User Research

Interviews showed that participants often rely on family members, Google Search, or Google Translate when they need help. They wanted translation to happen in one place, without switching between apps, and needed clearer support for understanding suspicious messages before interacting with them.
